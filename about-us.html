About Us
Paygeon was founded in 2020 issuing credit cards to small businesses affected by COVID-19. We successfully issued over $1M+ in credit to over 2,000 small businesses, now we aim to make credit available for everyone.
Through Paygeon, everyday businesses can embed credit into their platform in a way that is financially and socially rewarding. Borrowers apply online for a line of credit between $50,000 and $1,000,000. Businesses and institutions can embed these credit lines into their platform and own the customer journey. Paygeon handles all loan servicing on behalf of the matched borrowers and investors.

Each merchant is assigned a Merchant Category Code (“MCC”) that indicates the merchant's area of business. For example, hotels are generally assigned a specific MCC. We use MCCs to determine whether eligible purchases qualify for 2 Miles for each $1.00 spent. We do not assign or have any control over which MCC is assigned to a given merchant; the MCCs are chosen and assigned by a third party and that third party may change the MCCs from time to time. When you make a purchase at a merchant, we are provided the MCC for that merchant at the time of the transaction. If the MCC matches a category that gives you 2 Miles for each$1.00 spent - for example, if the MCC tells us that you made a purchase at a hotel - you will receive 2 Miles for each $1.00 spent for that purchase. You may believe that a purchase at a given merchant qualifies for 2 Miles, but we rely on the MCC applied to the merchant to make that determination. For example, if you eat at a restaurant within a hotel, it may be assigned a “restaurant” MCC, which will not earn 2 Miles for each $1.00 spent.
